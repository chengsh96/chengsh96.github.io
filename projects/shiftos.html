Moonwalkers Control System (ShiftOS 3.0 & 3.1)

Real-time control • Embedded systems • Sensing & estimation • Product deployment

Summary

  • Designed and deployed real-time control behaviors for wearable robotic shoes (Moonwalkers).
  • Improved stability and terrain handling via phase-aware logic and sensor fusion.
  • Integrated algorithms into an ARM Cortex RTOS stack for production.

What I did

  • Control architecture: acceleration shaping, braking logic, and safety constraints.
  • State/intent estimation: IMU + motor signals for phase-aware assistance.
  • Production integration: RTOS modules, data logging, tuning hooks, and reliability hardening.
