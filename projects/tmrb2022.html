Modeling Transitional Kinematics Between Walking & Stairs (TMRB 2022)

Optimization • Kinematic modeling • Smooth transitions

Summary

  • Unified kinematic representation spanning level walking, ramps, stair ascent/descent, and transitions.
  • Smooth interpolation using a transition coefficient (convex combination) between gait modes.
  • Optimization constraints for physically meaningful, low-jerk trajectories.
