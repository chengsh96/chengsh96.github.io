Continuous Control of Inter-Leg Transitions Across Activities (TNSRE 2024 / IROS 2023)

Continuous trajectories • Robust transitions • Real-time control

Summary

  • Continuous trajectory controller that avoids brittle discrete switching during walk↔stair transitions.
  • Handled inter-leg transition cases by phase-aligning and generating smooth references.
  • Improved robustness to timing errors in high-level classification.
